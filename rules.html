Server Rules

Last updated: June 3, 2025

Respect & Community

  1. Respect all players: Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated.
  2. Have fun and be kind: This is a community – let's keep it welcoming for everyone.
  3. Follow staff instructions: Moderators and admins have the final say in all disputes and decisions.

Fair Play & Cheating

  1. No cheating or exploiting: Use of hacks, mods, or bugs to gain unfair advantages is strictly prohibited.
  2. No griefing: Destroying or stealing other players' builds without permission is forbidden.
  3. No real-money trading: Buying, selling, or trading in-game items or ranks for real money outside official store is banned.

Chat & Communication

  1. Keep chat appropriate: Use respectful language; no spamming, advertising, or excessive profanity.
  2. Stay on-topic in forums and chats: Use designated channels for off-topic discussions.
  3. Report bugs and issues: Help us improve by reporting problems to the staff promptly.
